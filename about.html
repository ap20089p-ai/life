Why Blood Donation Matters

🩸

Save Lives

Every donation can save up to 3 lives. Blood transfusions are critical for surgery, trauma, and disease treatments.

⏱️

Quick Process

The donation process takes only 10-15 minutes. The entire visit is usually completed in about 30-45 minutes.

❤️

Easy & Safe

Donating blood is safe and simple. All equipment is sterile and used only once. You can donate every 8 weeks.

🌍

Universal Need

Blood is always needed. Emergencies, surgeries, and treatment of blood disorders create constant demand.
