How to Donate

1

Registration

Complete a brief registration form with your personal information.

2

Medical Screening

Answer health questions and have your blood pressure and temperature checked.

3

Donation

The actual donation takes 10-15 minutes. Lie back and relax while we collect your blood.

4

Recovery

Rest, enjoy refreshments, and receive your donor certificate. You're a hero!

🎁 Benefits of Giving Blood

  • ✅ Help save up to 3 lives with one donation
  • ✅ Get free health screening and blood test
  • ✅ Reduce risk of heart disease
  • ✅ Lower iron levels to prevent iron overload
  • ✅ Join a community of heroes
  • ✅ Receive donor certificates and rewards

✓ Eligibility Criteria

  • 🔹 Age: 18-65 years old
  • 🔹 Weight: At least 50 kg (110 lbs)
  • 🔹 Health: No major illnesses
  • 🔹 Blood Pressure: Normal range
  • 🔹 Hemoglobin: 12.5 g/dL or above
  • 🔹 No recent tattoos or piercings
