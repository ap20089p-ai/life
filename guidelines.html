Eligibility Guidelines

✓ You Can Donate If:

  • You are 18 years or older
  • You weigh at least 110 lbs (50 kg)
  • You are in good health
  • You have not donated blood in the last 8 weeks
  • You have a valid photo ID
  • Your blood pressure is within normal range

✗ You Cannot Donate If:

  • You have a fever or feel ill
  • You are pregnant or nursing
  • You have high blood pressure (>180/100)
  • You have recently had a tattoo or piercing
  • You are taking certain medications
  • You have a history of certain diseases

Preparation Tips:

  • Drink plenty of water and stay hydrated the day before
  • Eat a healthy meal 2-3 hours before donation
  • Get adequate sleep the night before
  • Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing
  • Keep your arms relaxed and warm
